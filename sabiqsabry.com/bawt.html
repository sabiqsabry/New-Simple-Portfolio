Bawt

Project Details

  • Category : Models
  • Created by : Sabiq Sabry
  • Product : bit.ly/bawt-behavioural-model

The Bawt model is an innovative application of Natural Language Processing (NLP) technology designed to assist individuals with Alexithymia, a condition that makes it challenging for them to express emotions. Through text-based communication on WhatsApp, Bawt enables these individuals to convey and express their emotions effectively. By analyzing linguistic patterns, sentiment indicators, and behavioral cues within text messages, Bawt can understand and identify the underlying emotional state of individuals with Alexithymia. The model aims to bridge the communication gap for these individuals, providing them with a means to express their feelings, experiences, and needs more accurately. Privacy and data security are prioritized during the development of Bawt, ensuring ethical considerations are met. Overall, the Bawt model empowers individuals with Alexithymia by enhancing their emotional expression and enabling a better understanding of their emotions through text-based communication.
